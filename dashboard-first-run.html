Repositories

What the first tick does

  1. Reads each repository above — open issues, PRs, and discussions you've asked it to watch — into a per-repo ledger.
  2. Works the queue: triage replies, PR reviews, delta re-reviews, and fix PRs, in priority order and within this tick's size limits.
  3. Stages everything for you under Ready for your final look (its merge shortlist) and Staged replies (everything else it drafted).
  4. Escalates the ties it shouldn't call alone — design direction, breaking changes, close/reject — under Decisions needed.
  5. Shows you the plan and the record: what it intends to do next tick, what it did last tick, and per-repo trends.

In draft mode the steward posts nothing — every outbound message waits for your click. Use the mode toggle and ⚙ Settings above to change mode, schedule, tick size, and what each repository is watched for. Repositories come from config.yaml.
